# KettOS Builder Certification Levels

The KettOS certification framework defines four builder levels. Each level unlocks new privileges in the platform and reflects demonstrated competency in AI solution building and governance.

---

## Level 1 — Explorer

**Who this is:** Someone who has completed AI literacy training and is exploring AI tools for the first time.

**How to achieve:**
- Complete KettOS AI Literacy Module (approx. 2 hrs)
- Submit at least one Intake Form (Gate 0)
- Attend one KettOS Builder Community session

**Privileges:**
- Access to the Agent Registry (read-only)
- Access to the Intake Form
- Access to all Docs
- Glean-powered search across all solutions

**What you cannot yet do:**
- Get a solution certified (must reach Builder level)
- Conduct peer reviews for others
- Submit solutions to the Agent Registry

**Recognition:**
- Explorer badge in the registry
- Listed in the Builder Community directory

---

## Level 2 — Builder

**Who this is:** Someone who has taken a real problem from intake through prototype and received peer review.

**How to achieve:**
- Hold Explorer level
- Complete Gates 0–3 on at least one solution
- Receive positive peer review sign-off

**Privileges:**
- All Explorer privileges
- Can conduct peer reviews for other Explorers and Builders
- Can submit solutions to Agent Registry (pending KettOS team certification at Gate 5)
- Can access Databricks sandbox environment

**What you cannot yet do:**
- Approve tools for the Act Now list
- Lead a Builder Study Group
- Certify solutions independently

**Recognition:**
- Builder badge in the registry
- Solution listed in Agent Registry with your name as Owner

---

## Level 3 — Architect

**Who this is:** Someone with multiple certified solutions and demonstrated ability to mentor others.

**How to achieve:**
- Hold Builder level
- Have at least 2 solutions certified at Gate 5 or 6
- Mentor at least one Explorer or Builder through Gates 0–3
- Complete KettOS Architecture Module (approx. 3 hrs)

**Privileges:**
- All Builder privileges
- Can recommend tools for Act Now / Watch / Ignore list (KettOS team approves)
- Can lead Builder Study Groups
- Can co-certify solutions with KettOS team at Gate 5
- Access to Databricks production model registry (read)

**Recognition:**
- Architect badge in the registry
- Featured in quarterly KettOS Innovation Showcase
- Invited to KettOS Advisory Circle (1x/quarter)

---

## Level 4 — Champion

**Who this is:** A recognized leader in the KettOS community who drives adoption, mentors at scale, and helps govern the platform itself.

**How to achieve:**
- Hold Architect level
- Have at least 5 solutions certified at Gate 5 or 6
- Run at least 2 Builder Study Groups per quarter
- Nominated by KettOS team and peer vote

**Privileges:**
- All Architect privileges
- Can approve tool additions to Act Now list (with KettOS team co-sign)
- Can represent KettOS in cross-functional stakeholder meetings
- Access to full Databricks production registry (read/write with oversight)
- Co-author of KettOS policy updates

**Recognition:**
- Champion badge in the registry
- Annual KettOS Champion Award
- Named in official KettOS communications
- Invited to KettOS Strategy Planning sessions

## Burnout Prevention: Mini-Moments & Micro-Moments

The KettOS platform automatically captures builder achievements to showcase learning and effort:

**Mini-Moments** (captured automatically):
- First intake form submitted
- First peer review given
- First pilot completed
- First solution certified

**Micro-Moments** (builder-curated):
- A surprising AI output that taught you something
- A failure that led to a better approach
- A user reaction to your solution

These are surfaced in Glean, shared in community standups, and stored in the registry to ensure builder effort is visible beyond just the final solution.
